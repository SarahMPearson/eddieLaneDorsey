ABOUT

Eddie Lane Dorsey was born and raised in the Midwest. She grew up at a time when parents encouraged their children to spend most of their days outside predominately unsupervised. Come the morning sun, she and her siblings would find themselves outside in the company of the neighborhood kids. Their imagination the only option for entertainment. Her summers were spent knee deep in the creek catching crawdads and riding her bike in search of an adventure. A dirty face was hard earned and skinned knees were proud battle scars. Winters were filled with sledding, snowball fights, and impromptu Army games that ended just after dusk. Frozen sneakers from falling into the creek was evidence of a game well played. On the days she was confined to the inside her time was spent reading and writing. This time well spent created a myriad of interests and skill sets following and flourishing into adulthood. Dorsey now resides in the hills of Eastern Tennessee. Most of her time is spent looking for the next adventure.
